about

I’m a researcher, designer, and community worker interested in meaning-making approaches that center relational entanglement, anti-coloniality, conviviality, low tech, mutual aid, and degrowth, among other ideas, as ways to sustain and care for the collective wellness of all beings.

I also make photographs.
