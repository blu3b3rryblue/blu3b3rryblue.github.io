Inspiration

We are inspired by LOW←TECH MAGAZINE, compost.party, Permacomputing as practice for digital Graphic Design, and Solar Protocol.

this website is built based on principles of permacomputing and relational aesthesis to reorient our relationship to digital technology toward more convivial and relational ways of being.
